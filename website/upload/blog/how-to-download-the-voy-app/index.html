How to download the Voy App

  • Hope James
  • July 09, 2019

Have you ever wondered about the stories behind your favorite building?


It is hard to truly explore a place without local knowledge. Most people need guidance navigating a new place or learning through locals. Voy app helps travelers learn through hearing locals’ stories and experiences.
App users can also give back by adding their owns stories to their favorite places for others to find. The app allow locals and tourists to explore their own neighborhoods, find hidden gems or share their own stories with others.

Voy app helps travelers learn through hearing locals’ stories and experiences.

Now to download Voy, follow the steps below :

For Apple Users

  1. Launch your App store or click here
  2. Search for Voy Audio Tour
  3. Tap Get the app for free.
  4. Launch the Voy App to start listening or creating your own stories.

For Android Users

  1. Launch your Play store or click here
  2. Search for Voy Audio Tour
  3. Tap install the app for free.
  4. Launch your Voy App to start listening.

Don’t forget to give a rating or review!
